Meet Rimamnuskep Haggai

CEO & Co-founder, RH Integrated Farm

Rimamnuskep Haggai is a visionary agripreneur dedicated to transforming the cassava industry in Nigeria and beyond. With a background in Science education, National Health Fellowship and as a passionate climate advocate for sustainable agriculture, Rimamnuskep drives innovation in cassava farming, prioritizing sustainability, community development, and economic empowerment.

Our Mission

RH Integrated Farm is a commercial cassava farming enterprise that supplies fresh cassava root tubers to food & beverage processors, starch & animal feed manufacturers, and local market women producing gari, abacha, fufu, and chips. In partnership with local farmers, we address hunger, malnutrition, joblessness, and poverty in Northern Nigeria and Africa.

Our Approach

We engage in all-season farming ensuring consistent productivity and sustainability. Our practices prioritize climate resilience, environmental safety and social responsibility. We cultivate cassava alongside rice, maize, yam and horticulture to promote diversified agriculture.

Community Development

Committed to community development, RH Integrated Farm empowers youths, women, and local settlers through modern farming techniques and improved market access. Initiatives foster economic growth, food security, and social stability.

Location

Our farms are located in Wukari LGA, Taraba State.
